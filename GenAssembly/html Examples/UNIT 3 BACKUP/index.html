We are Knot Excited

Our mission is to find and review the latest and greatest authentic garlic knot hotspots that Georgia has to offer. We feature new garlic knot reviews weekly ("Knot off the Press") and show you where and how to get them. We do not feature reviews from the general public nor do we recieve paid endorsements from any of the local businesses featured.
