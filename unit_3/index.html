Vincenza's Garlic Knots

An awe inspiring combination of fresh dough, garlic and parmesan accompanied by house-made marinara sauce. The quality and texture of these garlic knots pay homage to those found in Classic New York Pizzerias.

Alpharetta, GA

Rating: * * * * *

Ippolito's Garlic Rolls

These rolls are soft, buttery and fresh. Not quite doughy, but not overcooked. They are brushed with butter and come seasoned with parmesan and parsley. Highly recommended.

Alpharetta, GA

Rating: * * * * *

www.Ippolitos.net

We are Knot Excited

Our mission is to find and review the greatest garlic knots and rolls that Georgia has to offer and show you where and how to get them. We feature new reviews weekly ("Knot off the Press") and do not recieve paid endorsements from any of the local businesses featured.

If you know a place that makes awesome garlic knots or rolls in the Atlanta area and would like us to review them, please send us an email.
